FACILITY AGREEMENT

Dated: 3 March 2024

PARTIES

(1) PHOENIX CAPITAL PARTNERS LIMITED, a company incorporated in England 
    and Wales (company number 11234567), whose registered office is at 
    30 St Mary Axe, London, EC3A 8BF (the "Borrower");

(2) ATLAS CAPITAL BANK PLC, a public limited company incorporated in 
    England and Wales (company number 01234567), whose registered office 
    is at 25 Canary Wharf, London, E14 5LQ (the "Lender");

(3) STERLING TRUST CORPORATION LIMITED, acting as agent for the Lender 
    (the "Agent").

1. DEFINITIONS AND INTERPRETATION

"Facility" means a term loan facility in an aggregate amount of 
GBP 120,000,000 (one hundred and twenty million pounds sterling).

"Availability Period" means the period from the date of this Agreement 
to 3 June 2024.

"Final Maturity Date" means 3 March 2031.

"Interest Rate" means the aggregate of SONIA plus the Margin.

"Margin" means 2.75% per annum.

"Repayment Date" means each date falling at quarterly intervals after 
the first Utilisation Date.

"Commitment Fee" means 0.85% per annum of the undrawn Facility.

"Arrangement Fee" means GBP 1,200,000 payable on the date of this 
Agreement.

"Break Costs" means the amount certified by the Lender as being its 
loss incurred as a result of the Borrower repaying any Loan on a day 
other than a Repayment Date.

"Default Rate" means the Interest Rate plus 2.00% per annum.

2. THE FACILITY

2.1 Subject to the terms of this Agreement, the Lender makes available 
to the Borrower a sterling term loan facility in an aggregate amount 
equal to GBP 120,000,000.

2.2 The purpose of the Facility is to finance the acquisition of 
Harrington Office Developments Limited and to fund general corporate 
and working capital requirements of the Borrower.

3. UTILISATION

3.1 The Borrower may utilise the Facility during the Availability Period 
by delivering a Utilisation Request to the Agent not later than 
10:00 a.m. three Business Days before the proposed Utilisation Date.

3.2 The minimum amount of each Utilisation is GBP 10,000,000.

4. REPAYMENT

4.1 The Borrower shall repay the Facility in equal quarterly instalments 
of GBP 6,000,000 commencing three months after the first Utilisation Date.

4.2 The Borrower shall repay all outstanding amounts on the 
Final Maturity Date.

5. INTEREST

5.1 The rate of interest on each Loan for each Interest Period is 
the percentage rate per annum equal to SONIA plus 2.75% per annum.

5.2 Interest shall be paid on the last day of each Interest Period, 
which shall be three months unless otherwise agreed.

5.3 If the Borrower fails to pay any amount on its due date, interest 
shall accrue at the Default Rate from the due date until actual payment.

6. FEES

6.1 The Borrower shall pay to the Lender an Arrangement Fee of 
GBP 1,200,000 on the date of this Agreement.

6.2 The Borrower shall pay a Commitment Fee of 0.85% per annum 
calculated on the undrawn and uncancelled amount of the Facility, 
accruing daily from the date of this Agreement.

7. SECURITY

7.1 The obligations of the Borrower under this Agreement shall be 
secured by:
    (a) a first legal charge over the property at 
        12 Harbour Exchange Square, London, E14 9GE;
    (b) an assignment of rental income derived from the Property;
    (c) a debenture over all assets of the Borrower.

8. CONDITIONS PRECEDENT

8.1 The Lender is not obliged to comply with any Utilisation Request 
unless it has received the following documents:
    (a) constitutional documents of the Borrower;
    (b) a copy of the board resolution authorising the Facility;
    (c) evidence of insurance over the secured property;
    (d) a satisfactory valuation report from an approved valuer.

9. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

9.1 The Borrower represents and warrants that:
    (a) it is duly incorporated and validly existing;
    (b) it has the power to enter into this Agreement;
    (c) its obligations are legal, valid and binding;
    (d) no Event of Default is continuing.

10. FINANCIAL COVENANTS

10.1 The Borrower shall ensure that:
    (a) Loan to Value Ratio shall not exceed 60% tested quarterly;
    (b) Interest Coverage Ratio shall not be less than 2.50x tested 
        semi-annually;
    (c) Debt Service Coverage Ratio shall not be less than 1.50x 
        tested annually.

11. GENERAL UNDERTAKINGS

11.1 The Borrower undertakes that it shall not, without prior written 
consent of the Lender:
    (a) create or permit to subsist any security over its assets;
    (b) dispose of any material assets;
    (c) make any acquisitions exceeding GBP 5,000,000;
    (d) declare or pay any dividend while an Event of Default is continuing.

12. EVENTS OF DEFAULT

12.1 Each of the following is an Event of Default:
    (a) non-payment of any amount due under this Agreement;
    (b) breach of any financial covenant;
    (c) insolvency of the Borrower;
    (d) material adverse change in the financial condition of the Borrower;
    (e) cross-default on any financial indebtedness exceeding GBP 10,000,000.

12.2 On and at any time after the occurrence of an Event of Default, 
the Lender may cancel the Facility and declare all outstanding 
amounts immediately due and payable.

13. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

13.1 This Agreement and any dispute or claim arising out of or in 
connection with it shall be governed by and construed in accordance 
with the laws of England and Wales.

13.2 The parties irrevocably agree that the courts of England and Wales 
shall have exclusive jurisdiction to settle any dispute.
